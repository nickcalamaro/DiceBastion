## What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

## Why We Use Cookies

Dice Bastion uses cookies for the following purposes:

### 1. Essential Website Functionality
These cookies are necessary for our website to work properly. Without them, certain features would not be available:

- **Shopping Cart**: Remember items you've added to your cart
- **User Sessions**: Keep you logged in as you navigate the site
- **Security**: Protect against fraud and ensure secure connections
- **Cookie Preferences**: Remember your cookie consent choices

**These cookies cannot be disabled** as they are essential for the website to function.

### 2. Analytics and Performance (Optional)
With your permission, we use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website:

- Pages visited and content viewed
- Time spent on pages
- Navigation patterns
- Device and browser information (anonymized)
- General location (country/city level only)

This helps us:
- Improve website performance
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Understand which content is most valuable
- Make informed decisions about site improvements

## Cookies We Use

| Cookie Name | Type | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|------|---------|----------|
| `dicebastion_cookie_consent` | Essential | Stores your cookie preferences | 12 months |
| `cart_session` | Essential | Maintains your shopping cart | Session |

**Note**: Analytics cookies are only loaded if you give explicit consent.

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

### If you disable essential cookies:
- Shopping cart will not work
- You won't stay logged in
- Checkout process will fail
- Some pages may not load correctly

### If you disable analytics cookies:
- No impact on functionality
- We won't be able to improve the site based on usage data
- Your visit won't be counted in our statistics

## Gibraltar Compliance

As a business operating in Gibraltar, we comply with:
- UK GDPR (Gibraltar follows UK data protection law)
- Gibraltar Data Protection Act 2004
- Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations

Gibraltar does not have VAT, simplifying our cookie requirements for e-commerce compared to EU states.

## Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access**: Know what cookies we've set
- **Rectification**: Correct any inaccurate data collected
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent**: Change your cookie preferences at any time

## Technical Details

For developers and technically-minded users:

### Cookie Security
- All cookies are served over HTTPS
- HttpOnly flags set where appropriate
- SameSite attributes configured
- Secure flag enabled for sensitive cookies

### Data Minimization
We follow the principle of data minimization:
- Only collect necessary cookie data
- Anonymize analytics data
- Regular cookie audits
- Automatic cleanup of expired cookies

### Cookie Consent Management
Our consent management system:
- Blocks optional cookies until consent given
- Stores preferences locally (localStorage)
- Versions consent (re-ask if policy changes)
- Provides easy preference updates
